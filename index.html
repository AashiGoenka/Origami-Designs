Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • Chameleons live in warm varied habitats from rainforests through to deserts.
  • Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings.

Interesting Fact about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Teddy-Bear

  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  • Teddy bear is the best toy which can make us calm.
  • The teddy bear is a very traditional and timeless toy.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • Pandas have a great camouflage for their environment.
  • Pandas spend a lot of their day eating.

Interesting Facts about Flying-Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Most Cicadas have red-orange eyes.
  • Cicadas are also known for their buzzing and clicking noises.
